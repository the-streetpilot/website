About Me

I’m Varun Vinod, a 20-year-old Graphic Designer and Web Developer from Trivandrum, Kerala, with a deep passion for creativity and technology. As a partner at GRAFYO Design & Devs, I specialize in designing visually engaging, user-centric web experiences. Currently, I serve as the Webmaster of the IEEE Student Branch at Mohandas College of Engineering and Technology, where I was also the Design Lead from November 2023 to September 2024. My involvement extends further as the Secretary of the Computer Society of India (CSI) - Student Branch, where I focus on fostering innovation within the student community.

My freelance journey as a graphic designer and VFX artist began in May 2020, allowing me to collaborate with diverse clients on both creative and technical projects. Fluent in Hindi, Malayalam, and English, I’ve honed my skills in web development and server management, especially in the realm of GTA V and FIVEM server development, along with .lua editing. Over the past three years, I’ve also managed YouTube channels and Discord servers, gaining valuable experience in community management, moderation, and Discord.py bot development. I’ve built several custom bots for YouTubers, enhancing their audience engagement and automating their workflows.

My technical expertise is backed by a 2-month internship in Cloud Computing, focusing on AWS and Microsoft Azure. I hold certifications in Data Science (Python), Scientific Computing with Python, Cloud Computing from Cognizance, IIT Roorkee, and Product Management from TNX Learning. In addition, I have earned credentials in Java, JavaScript Algorithms and Data Structures, Project Management, Python Basics, and Responsive Web Design from freeCodeCamp, where I completed 300 hours of coding evaluation.

Throughout my journey, I’ve developed strong client management skills, ensuring seamless project execution and delivering high-quality results. Whether it’s design, development, or management, I’m always eager to take on new challenges and push the boundaries of what’s possible.
